Colored — see structure at a glance

A proofing aid for editorial workflows. Each imported style gets a distinct color via MD-Color-* swatches.

What Colored does

  • Assigns a unique color to every paragraph/character style
  • Creates MD-Color-* swatches in the Swatches panel
  • Makes H1, Body, lists, code, etc. instantly recognizable
  • Works on top of normal MD-* style names

Best practice

  • Enable while checking document structure
  • Turn off before final layout or PDF export
  • Import is slower with Colored enabled
  • Not available in Illustrator (by design)

Tip: Use Automatic import + Colored for a first pass, then refine typography with Colored off.
